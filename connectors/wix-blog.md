# Wix Blog

Connect your Wix blog to Scratch to edit and manage blog posts with AI.

## Authentication

Wix uses OAuth authentication:

- Click "Connect with Wix" and authorize access to your site

## What you can sync

- Blog posts and their content
- Post titles and metadata

## Getting started

1. Go to **Data Sources** in Scratch
2. Click **Add Connection** and select Wix Blog
3. Authorize access to your Wix site
4. Create a Workbook from your connection

## Supported field types

| Field | Support |
|-------|---------|
| Title | ✅ Read/Write |
| Content | ✅ Read/Write |
| Excerpt | ✅ Read/Write |
| SEO slug | ✅ Read/Write |
| Featured | ✅ Read/Write |

### Content format

Post content can be edited as HTML. Scratch converts between Wix's native format and HTML automatically.
